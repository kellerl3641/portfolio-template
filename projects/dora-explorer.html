Dora-The-Explorer Project

Context Mr. Mueller, my SEP 10 teacher, told us to make a project on any adventure that we can think of. This project requires a partner to work with. The title of Adventure is "Dora The Explorer. My partner and I created several different unique names for our character, but Swiper stood out the most to us.

Content

Our project is consist of 4 characters which are "Dora, Wiper, Diego and the Map" It has many links that can take you anywhere you choose and at the end of the game, there would be a surprise. Our Project was based on using links and using Github command lines in order to make the files and apply links to every file.

Self Reflection

My Teacher assigned this Projects for us to practice more with Github Command lines and The most challenging I have gone through was planning on what movie my partner and I should create. There's a million of ideas we can choose and sometimes it can be overwhelming. Another challenge I faced was collaborating with my partner outside of the school building. There were some times either my partner or myself would be occupied with something else. If I had to continue, I would have explored and went on with the story more.
